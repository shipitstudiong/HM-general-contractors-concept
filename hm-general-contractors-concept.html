Our Story

25 Years Building
Houston's Healthcare

HM General Contractors was founded in 2000 with one mission: to build medical and dental offices that reflect the quality of care delivered inside them. Led by Mike Newhouse and Eric Martinez, our team has become the go-to design-build contractor for healthcare professionals across Houston, Austin, and San Antonio.

We specialize in the unique demands of medical construction — from HVAC requirements and infection control to ADA compliance and equipment coordination. Our clients trust us because we've done this hundreds of times, and we do it right.

Whether you're starting a new practice from scratch or renovating an existing space, we manage the entire process — drawings, permits, construction, and vendor coordination — so you can focus on your patients.

"Our pledge to you is the highest quality medical construction, on time and on budget — with the least drama possible."
